## Product Overview
**Brand architecture:** Hububb is a proptech company building solutions for travel, living, and everything around stays, with a current focus on the flexible stays economy. Three products sit under it, each marketed on its own: **Host** (the operator app), **Stay** (guest-facing bookings), and **Work** (this file). Never market or imply a sibling product's capabilities.

**Positioning:** Hububb Work is the app for workers and service providers servicing these properties. Today it is focused on the core: cleaners, handymen, locksmiths, pest control, and other trades.

**Value proposition:** The work around stays, in one app. Providers service Hububb properties and the jobs those properties generate.

**Where it's going:** Potential to expand beyond the core trades, offering businesses the opportunity to provide many more services.

**Audience:** Workers and service providers servicing these properties. Cleaners, handymen, locksmiths, pest control, and other trades. Not the host, not the guest, the person doing the work.

## Target Audience
**Who they are:** Independent service providers and small local operators servicing short and mid-term stays: cleaners, handymen, locksmiths, pest control, and other trades.
**Decision-maker:** The provider, choosing where to spend their working hours.
**Primary use case:** Take on jobs servicing Hububb properties and get them done.

## Problems & Pain Points
- Unpredictable, feast-or-famine work and idle gaps between jobs.
- Unclear briefs: turning up without knowing the access, the scope, or the standard expected.
- Chasing payment and juggling scheduling across scattered messages.

## Competitive Landscape
*The real competitor is the fragmented status quo, not a rival app. Names are internal only and never appear in generated copy (use category descriptors like "the usual way of finding work," "the old way").*

| Job | What providers reach for today | Why it falls short | Why Hububb wins |
|-----|--------------------------------|--------------------|-----------------|
| Finding work | Word of mouth, scattered messages, juggling several clients | Feast-or-famine, idle gaps, unpredictable | Jobs from Hububb properties in one app |
| Knowing what to do | Turning up and figuring out access, scope, and standard on the spot | Unclear briefs, wasted trips, disputes | The job comes with the access, scope, and standard |
| Getting paid | Chasing invoices across clients | Slow, awkward, easy to lose track | Pay handled through the app |

## Differentiation
Hububb Work is one app for the work around stays. The provider services Hububb properties, takes on the jobs those properties generate, knows what each job needs, and gets paid, instead of running a scatter of clients, messages, and invoices. Starting with the core trades, with room to grow into a broader marketplace of services.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Will there be enough work | Jobs come from Hububb properties, not left to word of mouth. |
| Will I know what I'm walking into | The job comes with the access, scope, and standard up front. |
| Will I get paid on time | Payment runs through the app, no chasing invoices. |

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Feast-or-famine work, unclear briefs, wasted trips, chasing payment.
**Pull:** Jobs from Hububb properties, clear briefs, reliable pay, all in one app.
**Habit:** Word of mouth, my own client list, my own messages and invoices.
**Anxiety:** Depending on a platform for work and pay. To be addressed once positioning is confirmed.
