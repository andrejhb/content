## Product Overview
**Brand architecture:** Hububb is "Everything around Stays," the parent brand. Three products sit under it, each marketed on its own: **Host** (the operator app), **Stay** (guest-facing bookings), and **Work** (this file). Never market or imply a sibling product's capabilities.

**Positioning (provisional):** Hububb Work is the service-provider app, for the people who do the work around stays. Cleaners, handypeople, and local pros get jobs dispatched to them, know what to do, and get paid, all in one app.

**Audience:** Service providers in the Hububb network, cleaning partners, maintenance and handyperson pros, and other local services. Not the host, not the guest, the person doing the job.

*Everything below is a starting point drawn from the audience, to be confirmed. Do not treat as fact in creatives until reviewed.*

## Target Audience
**Who they are:** Independent service providers and small local operators, cleaners, handypeople, maintenance pros, who take on jobs around short-term stays.
**Decision-maker:** The provider, choosing where to spend their working hours.
**Primary use case:** Receive dispatched jobs, know exactly what's needed, and get paid reliably.

## Problems & Pain Points *(provisional)*
- Unpredictable, feast-or-famine work and idle gaps between jobs.
- Unclear briefs: turning up without knowing the access, the scope, or the standard expected.
- Chasing payment and juggling scheduling across scattered messages.
