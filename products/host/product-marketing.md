## Product Overview
**Brand architecture:** Hububb is "Everything around Stays," the parent brand. Three products sit under it, each marketed on its own: **Host** (this file), **Stay** (guest-facing bookings, a separate product), and **Work** (the service-provider app, a separate product). Never market or imply a sibling product's capabilities.

**Positioning:** Your autonomous AI host. The general AI host that runs your property end to end. Bookings, guests, cleaning, and pricing, all handled while you watch.

**One-liner:** Everything a host needs, all in one app.

**Value proposition:** Automate your hosting. The shift is real: not a Host OS you operate, but autonomous hosting that runs itself while you stay in control.

**Category:** The AI host. The app that runs your property for you, not another tool you have to drive.

**Live today:** unified inbox, multi-calendar, multi-channel distribution (Airbnb, Booking, Vrbo), AI guest messaging agent (Hostie), direct booking website, dynamic pricing, smart access arrangements, automated cleaning (dispatched the moment a guest checks out), services marketplace, guest portal, and a performance dashboard. Most of what was once roadmap is shipped.

## Target Audience
**Who they are:** Owner-operator short-term rental hosts, from the 1-property side-hustler to the roughly 20-unit operator.
**Where:** London first, then the UK, then phased global. London is the focus for now.
**Decision-maker:** The host. An owner-operator purchase, no committee.
**Primary use case:** Automate the running of the property end to end while keeping control.

**Jobs to be done:**
- Have the property run itself: bookings, guest messages, cleaning, pricing, and access, all handled.
- Stay in control without doing the work: adjust what the AI knows and how it speaks.
- Earn more with less effort: higher earnings and fewer fires.
- Get out of the WhatsApp-and-spreadsheets chaos and the 9 a.m. group chats.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Running a short-term rental by hand quietly becomes a stressful, disconnected second job. The host is the operating system: chasing cleaners, living in WhatsApp and spreadsheets, putting out fires, fielding 9 a.m. group chats.

**Why alternatives fall short:** Most hosts run it manually, with disconnected setups: a spreadsheet, WhatsApp, one trusted cleaner, gut feel. Occasionally there is old, clunky PMS software, but that is a net of non-proprietary tools that are paid for separately and, even when they talk to each other, get lost in translation. The effort to make sense of it is not worth it.

**What it costs them:** Time (always on), money (silent leaks, revenue left on the table), mental load (the rental lives in their head), and risk (missed cleans, no-shows, bad reviews).

**Emotional tension:** The passive income that became a full-time job. What they want is to be in control without being involved in everything.

## Differentiation
Hububb is the interconnected app that combines cleaning, messaging, pricing, access arrangements, property guides, and more under one roof, all handled by an AI agent, so the host can rest instead of clicking through a complex UI. Every other option is a net of separate tools you pay for and stitch together; even when they speak to each other it gets lost in translation and the effort is not worth it. Here, everything is under one roof, properly automated by your AI host, and cleanly presented in a reports dashboard.
