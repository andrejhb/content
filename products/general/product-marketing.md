# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 6 Jul 2026*
*Scope: Hububb (parent brand) only. This is the company / vision hub that powers the @wearehububb account. It is the one place the three products are named and tied together. Source: hububb.com/about (founder manifesto) + brand architecture. No em-dashes.*

## Product Overview
**Brand architecture:** Hububb is the parent brand, "everything around stays." Three products sit under it, each marketed on its own: **Host** (the operator app), **Stay** (guest-facing bookings), and **Work** (the service-provider app). This "general" hub is the parent voice. Unlike the three products, which must never imply a sibling's capabilities, the parent hub is the single place where all three are named together and shown as one ecosystem. That is its entire job.

**Positioning:** The invisible layer beneath the flexible stays economy. Hububb is the engine that lets the whole thing run quietly, tying the host, the guest, and the worker into one moving system.

**Brand line:** For the feeling of home, everywhere you go.

**One-liner:** Everything around stays.

**Value proposition:** We believe living anywhere should feel like coming home. A place that meets you where you are, runs itself while you are in it, and asks nothing of you but that you live. Hububb is the operational backbone and the hands on the ground that make that true.

**Category:** Proptech building the invisible operational layer beneath the flexible stays economy. Not another dashboard, but the software and the hands together.

**The three forms:**
- **Hububb Host** automates the whole of hosting: answering guests, listing you everywhere, booking the cleans, and handling what needs handling before you think of it.
- **Hububb Stay** opens the door to fully managed, mid-term stays in tech-enabled homes, where everything is taken care of. You book once and then you let go.
- **Hububb Work** turns scattered gigs into real, reliable work for the people who keep it all running, with far more to come.

**Vision tease policy:** Tease the future lightly, as "creative, innovative solutions" and "disrupting the industry." Do not name unshipped sub-products or future offerings (held back for now). Speak to what is real: the ecosystem, the three products, London-first, and the physical network of real people.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** A new way of living has taken shape, somewhere between a hotel and a lease, and it happened faster than anyone built for. People move for a season, work from another city, make a home of a place they have never been. Living anywhere should feel like coming home. Right now it asks for far too much.

**Where the effort lands today:**
- **The host improvises,** stitching five apps into a system held together by memory and worry.
- **The people who do the real work,** the cleaning, the fixing, the turning over of rooms, chase one-off jobs with no steady pipeline.
- **The guest arrives hopeful and unsure,** because a photograph is a promise nobody has to keep.

**What it costs:** Effort is everywhere. Ease is nowhere. The economy runs on friction that no single tool has removed, because no one has built for all three sides at once.

**Emotional tension:** A quiet revolution in how the world lives, running on improvisation. The feeling of home, promised everywhere and delivered almost nowhere.

## Differentiation
Hububb is the invisible layer beneath the flexible stays economy, the engine humming quietly beneath every stay. Two things make it different. First, it is built for all three sides at once, host, guest, and worker, as one system, not one product blind to the others. Second, it started with the physical side, not just the software: a vetted network of real people, proven on real properties, because anyone can tell you what needs doing and only a person can do it well. The software and the hands, together.
